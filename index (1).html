Shipping & Returns

Delivery

We ship across Nigeria. Standard delivery takes 2–5 business days. Lagos orders are typically delivered within 24–48 hours.

  • Lagos: ₦1,000 — 1–2 business days
  • Other states: ₦1,500 — 3–5 business days
  • Free shipping on orders above ₦15,000

Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

To initiate a return, contact us at hello@plain.co or via WhatsApp with your order number.

Exchanges

Size exchanges are free on your first exchange per order. We'll cover the cost of the return shipping label.

Size Guide

All measurements are in inches. We recommend measuring your chest and comparing to the chart below.

Classic & V-Neck Tees

  • XS — Chest: 34–36", Length: 26"
  • S — Chest: 36–38", Length: 27"
  • M — Chest: 38–40", Length: 28"
  • L — Chest: 40–42", Length: 29"
  • XL — Chest: 42–44", Length: 30"
  • XXL — Chest: 44–46", Length: 31"

Oversized Drop

The Oversized Drop runs 2 sizes up by design. If you're between sizes, size down. The drop shoulder falls approximately 2" below the natural shoulder.

How to Measure

Wrap a tape measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping it parallel to the ground. Add 1–2 inches for a relaxed fit.

Care Instructions

Washing

  • Machine wash cold (30°C max)
  • Turn inside out before washing
  • Use a gentle cycle
  • Do not bleach

Drying

  • Tumble dry on low heat or air dry flat
  • Avoid high heat — it may cause shrinkage
  • Reshape while damp for best results

Ironing

  • Iron on medium heat if needed
  • Do not iron directly on print or embroidery (if applicable)

Storage

Fold and store flat, or hang on a wide-shoulder hanger to preserve the shape. Avoid cramped spaces that can distort the fabric.
